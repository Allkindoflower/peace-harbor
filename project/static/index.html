Home Page

Welcome. You're probably bothered by something in your life if you're here. This place is to calm your emotions, mind and thoughts. Kind of like a mental well-being corner.

Mood Logging

The "Mood Logger" encourages you to log however you feel right now. By logging more moods when you feel like it, you can see how your mood trends over days and weeks.

Wins

The "Win Tracker" wants you to log your "wins" you have done lately. Any positive action in your life can be a win, even throwing out the trash or cooking a simple breakfast for yourself instead of starving.

Journal

This is your journal. You are allowed to log whatever is in your mind right now. Typing down your thoughts can help bring order in your mind instead of struggling with several anxiety-inducing thoughts.
